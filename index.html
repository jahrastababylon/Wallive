How it works

For everyone.
Two ways in.

For visitors & tourists

Explore the city in AR

1

Open wallive.app on your phone — no download needed. Works in your browser.

2

Point your camera at any WALLIVE-enabled mural, landmark, or artwork.

3

Watch it come alive — animation, story, history, or a hidden AR experience appears instantly.

4

Follow AR routes — curated walks that turn any city into an interactive experience.

Try it now →
For museums, cities & brands

Tell your story in AR

1

Upload a photo of your mural, building, or exhibition through our no-code CMS dashboard.

2

Add AR content — video, animation, audio, story. Do it yourself or order production from our team.

3

Go live instantly — your content appears in WALLIVE for all visitors in your city.

4

Track engagement — see how many people scanned your location, for how long, and from where.
